About

The short version

I’m a software engineer who designs and builds full stack web applications, AI driven data pipelines, and financial software, and designs and writes them from concept to finished product. A background in biochemistry taught me to turn messy, real world data into clear decisions. Years of professional creative work taught me to keep the person on the other side of the screen in view.

I design every platform I build, not only the code behind it, and I work comfortably across React, Next.js, Python, PostgreSQL, REST APIs, and LLM powered NLP. I care about user centered design, scalable architecture, and software that is as clear to use as it is to build.
