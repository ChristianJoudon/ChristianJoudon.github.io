About

The short version

Most of my work sits where software meets money and operations: trust accounting, reservations, invoice pipelines, and the internal tools a business runs on. It usually takes the same shape, taking a process that lives in spreadsheets and scattered steps and turning it into something the people inside it can actually use. I design the whole product, not just the code behind it, and I care as much about how it reads and feels as about how it runs.
